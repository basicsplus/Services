A Dull Knife

The biggest problem in home kitchens is dull knives. A sharp knife does a better job of cutting and slicing. This makes it easy to control how the blade moves through the food. A dull knife will slip and could plunge into your fingers. A sharp knife is always safer and keeping a sharp edge on your knives is really important.
